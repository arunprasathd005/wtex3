What is the main purpose of organ donation?

Donation is the giving of an organ and tissue to help someone that needs a transplant. Transplants can save or transform the life of a person. One organ and tissue donor can help transform the lives of more than 10 people. This relies on donors and their families agreeing to donate their organ and tissue after death.

What organs can you share?

Only a few organs can be transplanted from a living person to another. A living donor can donate one of their kidneys, or a portion of their lung, liver, pancreas or intestine. Living kidney donation is the most common living donation and helps save thousands of lives each year.
